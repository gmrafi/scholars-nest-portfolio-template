# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This personal portfolio project (www.gmrafi.com.bd) is maintained by Md Golam Mubasshir Rafi as a professional academic portfolio. While this is primarily a personal project showcasing research, education, and professional achievements, we are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for anyone who wishes to interact with this project.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

* **Respectful Communication**: Being respectful and professional in all interactions
* **Constructive Feedback**: Providing thoughtful, helpful feedback on content or technical issues
* **Academic Integrity**: Respecting intellectual property rights and providing proper attribution
* **Professional Conduct**: Maintaining professional standards in discussions and contributions
* **Privacy Respect**: Respecting the personal information and privacy of the portfolio owner

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

* **Harassment**: Any form of harassment, intimidation, or offensive comments
* **Plagiarism**: Copying or redistributing original content without permission
* **Impersonation**: Falsely representing yourself as the portfolio owner
* **Spam**: Posting irrelevant, promotional, or spam content in issues/discussions
* **Disrespect**: Trolling, insulting comments, or personal attacks
* **Copyright Infringement**: Violating the copyright and intellectual property rights outlined in LICENSE.md

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- GitHub Issues and Pull Requests
- Discussions and comments on this repository
- Email communications related to this project
- Any public interaction related to www.gmrafi.com.bd

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:
1. **Warning**: A private warning explaining the violation
2. **Temporary Block**: Temporary restriction from interacting with the repository
3. **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from the project and blocking from future interactions
4. **Legal Action**: In cases of copyright infringement or harassment, legal action may be pursued under Bangladesh law

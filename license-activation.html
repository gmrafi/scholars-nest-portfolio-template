License Activation Required

To use The Scholar's Nest template, you need to activate your license.

How to Activate Your License

  1. Purchase The Scholar's Nest template from Lemon Squeezy
  2. After purchase, you'll receive a license key via email
  3. Download the template files and extract them
  4. Open config.js in a text editor
  5. Replace "YOUR_LICENSE_KEY_HERE" with your actual license key
  6. Save the file and refresh your browser
